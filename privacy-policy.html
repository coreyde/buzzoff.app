Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

While using Our Service, We may ask You to provide Us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify You. Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Email address
  • First name and last name
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Authentication information from Third‑Party Sign‑In Providers (for example, when You sign in with Apple or Google)

Health and Habit Data (User‑Entered)

The core purpose of Buzz Off is to help You reduce or quit caffeine. To provide this functionality, You may choose to enter the following types of data:

  • Daily check‑ins (mood, fatigue, headaches, irritability, cravings, low mood, and whether You had caffeine)
  • Caffeine intake logs (for example, type of drink and amount of caffeine)
  • Your goals and preferences (for example, Cold Turkey or Gradual mode, reasons for quitting, symptoms and goals selected during onboarding)
  • Estimated daily caffeine amount (for example, in milligrams per day)

This information is entered by You and is used to power features such as streaks, progress charts, achievements, insights, and tools designed to support Your caffeine reduction journey. We do not sell this data.

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service.

Usage Data may include information such as Your Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, crash information, in‑app events, and other diagnostic data that help Us understand how the Service is used and how it performs.

This data is used to maintain and improve the stability, performance, and user experience of the Service.

Information from Third‑Party Sign‑In Providers

The Company allows You to create an account and log in to use the Service through the following Third‑Party Sign‑In Providers:

You may also have the option of sharing additional information from Your provider account. If You choose to provide such information and Personal Data, You are giving the Company permission to use, share, and store it in a manner consistent with this Privacy Policy.
